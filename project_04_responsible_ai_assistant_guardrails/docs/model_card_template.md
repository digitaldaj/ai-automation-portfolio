## Guardrails

- Requires source-grounded answers for policy or process guidance.
- Asks clarifying questions when prompts are ambiguous.
- Avoids guarantees or commitments.
- Routes high-risk or compliance-sensitive topics to human review.
- Provides citations or source references where available.

## Monitoring plan

- Review failed prompts monthly.
- Update test cases when procedures change.
- Track recurring unsupported questions.
- Maintain ownership for knowledge articles.

## Known limitations

- May misinterpret ambiguous questions.
- May need current source material to answer accurately.
- Should not replace human review for high-risk situations.
